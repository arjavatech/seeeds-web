About Us

VISON STATEMENT

                      Uplift the economically challenged children to progress in education irrespective of gender,
                      caste, religion,race, color & language so that they can be self-reliant.

                      SEEEDS strongly believes that economically backward children can become self-reliant
                      through education
            ​

WHAT WE DO

                       SEEEDS was established to ensure that every child in the country has access to education, irrespective of
                       their background or family income. To achieve this, we identify deserving children and we support them till
                       they become self-reliant through education.
                        
                       We accomplish that by offering them financial assistance and also by delivering range of educational
                       resources to raise their standard to compete and attain their goal in the field of their choice.
                        
                       Presently we are supporting about 400+ students to pursue higher education of their choice like Engineering,
                       Medicine, Agriculture, Charted Accountant (like CPA in USA), Nursing,etc. They are currently studying at
                       reputed institutions like Madras Medical College, Anna University, PSG Tech, CIT, etc. Some of the students
                       have an ambition to do Civil Services - IAS/IPS.

                       We constantly strive to identify deserving children who cannot afford to study further of their choice. We
                       screen students from government schools based on the merit and economic condition of the family.
                        ​
                       We primarily have two branches USA & India and planning to open in other countries as well.
                        ​
                       We will make available our form 990 Tax returns and State annual reports available to anyone upon request.
            ​

VISION STATEMENT

Uplift the economically challenged children to progress in education irrespective of gender, caste, religion,race,color & language so that they can be self-reliant. SEEEDS strongly believes that economically backward children can become self-reliant through education ​

WHAT WE DO

SEEEDS was established to ensure that every child in the country has access to education, irrespective of their background or family income. To achieve this, we identify deserving children and we support them till they become self-reliant through education. We accomplish that by offering them financial assistance and also by delivering range of educational resources to raise their standard to compete and attain their goal in the field of their choice. Presently we are supporting about 400+ students to pursue higher education of their choice like Engineering, Medicine, Agriculture, Charted Accountant (like CPA in USA), Nursing,etc. They are currently studying at reputed institutions like Madras Medical College, Anna University, PSG Tech, CIT, etc. Some of the students have an ambition to do Civil Services - IAS/IPS. We constantly strive to identify deserving children who cannot afford to study further of their choice. We screen students from government schools based on the merit and economic condition of the family. ​ We primarily have two branches USA & India and planning to open in other countries as well. ​ We will make available our form 990 Tax returns and State annual reports available to anyone upon request. ​
