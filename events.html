SEEEDS CONNECT

This is SEEEDS’ first step in its remarkable journey. The main purpose of this event is to celebrate the outstanding group of students we have and to encourage them to grow greater heights. We also would like to acknowledge the donors, volunteers and mentors who have been the pillars of SEEEDS in many ways. This journey wouldn’t have been possible without them. We have also invited the parents and guardians of every SEEEDS students and we will recognise them for allowing their children to pursue their dream. We will use this event as a platform to attract more volunteers and donors as there are hundreds and thousands of students who have great dreams but needs a supporting hand to fulfil them. And SEEEDS will always be a ladder to every underprivileged student.
