Admissions

OUR SELECTION PROCESS

  • We start accepting scholarship applications after 12th (+2) results
  • Short list the applications by applying our criteria (>80% & Studied in Govt/Govt Aided schools)
  • Conduct House verification for shortlisted students
  • Invite students for Face to face interview
    • For those who cannot afford travel expenses, we cover the expenses
  • Final selection of the students based on various factors
  • Preference given to Orphans & Sri Lankan Refugees
  • For Medical courses, we also send volunteers to the counselling to identify a deserving poor students.
